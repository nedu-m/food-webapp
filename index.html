About us

Amah's Kitchen is aimed at those who want to connect and rediscover their roots, as well as for those who want to know more about Nigerian culinary customs and traditions. With our cuisine with traditional ingredients you can experience an explosion of flavors on the palate which will transport you to the experiences of childhood or will make you know the culture, seasoning and Nigerian flavor. A place suitable for all cultures to meet the iconic dishes of our cuisine, inviting them to live an unprecedented Nigerian experience.
